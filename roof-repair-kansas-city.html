Roof Repair Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions Kansas City homeowners have about roof repairs, helping you understand when and why repairs are needed.

How do I know if my roof needs repairs?

Common signs include water stains on ceilings or walls, missing or damaged shingles, curled or cracked shingles, granules in gutters, daylight visible through roof boards, or sagging roof areas. Even if you don't see obvious problems, it's wise to have professional inspections after severe storms or every few years. Early detection and repair of small problems prevents major damage and expensive repairs later.

How long do roof repairs typically take?

Most roof repairs can be completed in a few hours to a full day, depending on the complexity and extent of damage. Simple shingle replacements or flashing repairs are often done the same day. More extensive repairs may require 2-3 days. We provide realistic timelines during our assessment and prioritize emergency repairs that protect your home from immediate damage.

Should I repair or replace my roof?

This depends on your roof's age, extent of damage, and cost-effectiveness. Generally, if repair costs exceed 30% of replacement cost, or if you're frequently needing repairs, replacement may be more economical. For roofs under 15 years old with localized damage, repairs are usually the best choice. We provide honest assessments and help you make the most cost-effective decision for your situation.

Will my homeowners insurance cover roof repairs?

Coverage depends on the cause of damage and your specific policy. Storm damage, sudden accidents, and covered perils are typically covered, while gradual wear and maintenance issues usually aren't. We help document damage properly and work with insurance adjusters to ensure covered repairs are approved. We'll also clearly explain what's covered versus what might be your responsibility.

Can I do roof repairs myself?

While some minor maintenance can be done safely, most roof repairs require professional expertise and equipment for safety and effectiveness. Improper repairs can void warranties, create bigger problems, and pose serious safety risks. Professional repairs ensure proper diagnosis, quality materials, correct installation techniques, and warranty protection. The cost of professional repair is typically much less than fixing DIY mistakes.

What warranty do you provide on roof repairs?

We provide warranties on both materials and workmanship for all roof repairs. Material warranties vary by manufacturer and product type, while our workmanship warranty covers our installation and repair work. We'll explain all warranty terms clearly and provide documentation. Our goal is your complete satisfaction, and we stand behind our work with confidence in our repair quality and durability.

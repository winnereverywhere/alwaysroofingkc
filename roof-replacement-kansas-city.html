Roof Replacement Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions Kansas City homeowners have about roof replacement, helping you make informed decisions about your home's protection.

How do I know if I need a roof replacement or just repairs?

Several factors determine whether replacement is necessary: age of your roof (most asphalt shingles last 20-25 years), extent of damage, frequency of repair needs, and cost-effectiveness. If you're experiencing multiple leaks, extensive shingle damage, or your roof is over 20 years old, replacement is often more economical long-term. Our thorough inspection will help you make the most informed decision for your situation and budget.

What's the typical timeline for a complete roof replacement?

Most residential roof replacements take 2-5 days depending on size, complexity, weather conditions, and materials being installed. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and keep you informed throughout the process. Our experienced crew works efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards to minimize disruption to your daily routine and protect your home during the replacement process.

How much does a roof replacement typically cost?

Roof replacement costs vary based on size, materials chosen, complexity, and any structural upgrades needed. We provide detailed, transparent estimates that break down all costs so you understand exactly what you're paying for. While replacement is a significant investment, quality materials and professional installation provide decades of protection and can increase your home's value substantially.

What happens to my old roofing materials?

We handle complete removal and proper disposal of all old roofing materials as part of our service. This includes shingles, underlayment, flashing, and any damaged decking. We ensure your property is left clean and all materials are disposed of responsibly according to local regulations. This complete tearoff approach ensures the best foundation for your new roof installation.

What kind of warranty comes with a roof replacement?

Your roof replacement includes comprehensive warranty coverage including manufacturer warranties on materials (typically 25-50 years) and our workmanship warranty covering installation. We explain all warranty terms in detail and provide everything in writing. Our reputation depends on your long-term satisfaction, so we stand behind every aspect of your roof replacement with confidence.

Can I stay in my home during the replacement?

Yes, most homeowners stay in their homes during roof replacement. While there will be noise and activity, we take measures to minimize disruption and protect your property. We'll discuss what to expect and provide recommendations for protecting valuables and planning around the work schedule. Our professional team works efficiently and respects your home throughout the replacement process.
